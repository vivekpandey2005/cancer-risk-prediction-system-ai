Cancer Precautions

Simple habits that help reduce cancer risk 🧠

🥗 Healthy Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and fiber-rich foods. Avoid junk and processed food.

🏃 Regular Exercise

Do at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily like walking, yoga, or gym.

🚭 Avoid Smoking

Smoking and tobacco are major causes of cancer. Avoid them completely.

🧘 Stress Control

Reduce stress using meditation, proper sleep, and relaxation techniques.

🏥 Regular Checkups

Go for regular medical checkups for early detection and prevention.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water daily to keep your body healthy and remove toxins.
